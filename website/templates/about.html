But with all the school work and daily home chores, things weren't that easy. We kept in our minds that: Everything we run away from has power over us, everything we go through, we conquer.   • The Solution Challenge - GDSC

    During this event, each university hosting such a community was to propose a solution to a real life problem. On our side, we decided to solve the problem of change when paying taxi trips and we named it MyTPS. So, that was our solution we proposed to the challenge. We highly believed that we'll be among the top in the Google ranking.

Though it was not that easy to manage between school work and the project (MyTPS), all we wanted is to give our best in everything.
